About RTU Pass

An idea which transformed into Website.

  • August 2015

    An Idea Emerged...

    We were searching for subect notes (as per RTU syllabus). But ther wasn't a single website which provided us the matter we needed. So we decided to make a website, by ourselves, which can provide Supreme quality notes, Laboratory Manuals and previous year exam papers(All at a single place) to students like us.

  • September 2015

    Planning Started

    This was a difficult task. Collecting and creating such huge amount and number of resources is never easy. But by productive planning and persistant hard work, the aim looked possible to be achieved!!

  • October'15 - February'16

    We Started Gathering Matrial

    Humongous task this was!! Still due to good planning, hard work and support from friends and teachers, We continued to move forward.

  • March 2016

    Humble Beginnig

    Its always special when, what you think becomes reality. Here we are, for every student of RTU , with this beautiful website RTU Pass!!

  • By October'16

    Transition to Full Service

    We're planning to add some more branches and launch and android app for the same. We'll soon be provinding more efficient notes. Till than keep encouraging us, your support is what we will always be looking for.

  2. Inform your classmates and teachers about RTU Pass and help promoting us on Facebook by sharing the page on your profile.

  4. Finally, mail us again attaching your college notice board as a proof.
